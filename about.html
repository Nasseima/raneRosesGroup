About Us

Welcome to RANE, where passion blooms in every petal and each blossom tells a story. Nestled in the heart of The bronx, we are more than just a flower shop; we are curators of nature's most exquisite art form: roses.

At RANE, we believe in celebrating life's moments, big or small, with the timeless elegance of roses. Whether you're expressing love, gratitude, or sympathy, our meticulously curated collection offers the perfect bouquet for every occasion.
